## IMF Staff-Level Agreement: What Changed and Why
The International Monetary Fund (IMF) announced on Saturday that it had reached a staff-level agreement with Pakistan to unlock a $1.2 billion tranche. This development is a significant boost to the Pakistan economy today, as it provides much-needed foreign exchange reserves to support the country's economic stability. The agreement is subject to approval by the IMF's Executive Board, which is expected to meet in the coming weeks. According to the IMF, the staff-level agreement is a key step towards completing the third review of Pakistan's Extended Fund Facility (EFF) arrangement. The EFF is a loan program designed to support countries facing balance of payments difficulties.

The IMF's decision to unlock the $1.2 billion tranche is a positive development for Pakistan, as it will help to support the country's economic recovery.
