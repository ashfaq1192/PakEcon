## Who Must File and Required Documents
In Pakistan, salaried individuals, business owners, and freelancers must file their income tax return. ## Step-by-Step Filing Process and Deadline Dates
The step-by-step process for filing an income tax return using the FBR IRIS portal is straightforward. First, create an account on the IRIS portal by registering your NTN and CNIC. Then, fill out the tax return form, which includes providing your income details, deductions, and tax payments. Once you've completed the form, submit it and pay any due tax. The deadline for filing an income tax return in Pakistan is typically around September of each year, but it's best to check the FBR website for the exact date. ## Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What are the benefits of being a tax filer in Pakistan?
A: As a tax filer, you can enjoy benefits such as lower withholding tax rates, ability to purchase property, and increased creditworthiness. Additionally, tax filers are also eligible for refunds if they have overpaid their taxes.
Q: What are the common mistakes to avoid when filing an income tax return?
A: Common mistakes include incorrect or incomplete information, missing documents, and late filing. To avoid these mistakes, ensure you have all the required documents, double-check your form for errors, and file your return before the deadline.
Q: How can I check my income tax return status?
A: You can check your income tax return status by logging into your IRIS account and clicking on the "Return Status" tab. This will show you the current status of your return, including any pending actions or notifications.
