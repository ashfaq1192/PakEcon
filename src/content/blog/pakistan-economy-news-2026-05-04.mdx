## Ministries Directed to Surrender Unspent Funds
The government has taken a significant step towards optimizing public finance management by directing all ministries to surrender their unspent funds. This move is part of the preparations for the upcoming budget, where the government aims to ensure that all allocated funds are utilized efficiently. The directive is expected to have a positive impact on the Pakistan economy today, as it will help in reducing waste and misallocation of resources. According to reports, the government is keen on streamlining its financial operations to achieve better outcomes.
