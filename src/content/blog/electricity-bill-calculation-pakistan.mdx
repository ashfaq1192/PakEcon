## How DISCOs Apply Tariffs and Calculate Bills
Distribution Companies (DISCOs) such as LESCO, KESC, and MEPCO are responsible for applying the NEPRA tariff slabs to calculate electricity bills for household consumers. The bill calculation involves fixed charges, which are a one-time payment for the meter and other services, and variable charges, which are based on the amount of electricity consumed. Fuel adjustment charges, which are adjusted quarterly, are also added to the bill to account for changes in fuel prices. Taxes and surcharges, such as the GST and FED, are also included in the bill. It's essential to understand how to read your electricity bill to identify the different components and take steps to reduce your consumption. You can visit the NEPRA website (https://www.nepra.org.pk) for more information on the tariff slabs and bill calculation process.
