## Budget 2026-27: Steeper Fines Introduced
The Budget 2026-27 has introduced steeper fines for tax compliance, aiming to encourage more people to pay their taxes on time. This move is expected to boost the government's revenue and promote a culture of tax compliance in the country. According to the Dawn Business report, the new fines will be imposed on individuals and businesses that fail to file their tax returns or pay their taxes on time. The State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) and the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) will work together to implement this new policy and ensure that taxpayers comply with the regulations.

The introduction of steeper fines is a significant step towards improving tax compliance in Pakistan. The country has struggled with low tax-to-GDP ratio, and this move is expected to help increase revenue and reduce the budget deficit. The FBR has been working to simplify the tax filing process and make it more accessible to taxpayers, and the new fines will provide an additional incentive for people to pay their taxes on time.
