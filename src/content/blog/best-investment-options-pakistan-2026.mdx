National Savings Pakistan offers several investment options, including Behbood, RIC, and DSC, which provide a fixed income and are considered low-risk investments. ## Other Investment Options in Pakistan
For those who are willing to take on higher risk, the stock market (PSX) and real estate are other investment options in Pakistan. The stock market offers the potential for high returns, but it can be volatile, and investors should be prepared for fluctuations. Real estate investment, on the other hand, can provide a steady income through rental yields and long-term capital appreciation. Gold investment is another option, where you can buy gold coins or bars and hold them as a store of value.
