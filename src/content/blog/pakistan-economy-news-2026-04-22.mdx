### Impact on Pakistan's Economy

## Attock Refinery Shuts Down
### Disruption to Crude Supply
Attock Refinery has shut down its main distillation unit due to road closures disrupting crude supply. According to reports, the road closures were linked to the arrival of foreign delegates in Islamabad, which halted tanker movement and cut crude receipts. The shutdown is expected to have a significant impact on Pakistan's oil supply, particularly on the availability of petrol and diesel. The country is already facing a significant shortage of oil, and the shutdown of the Attock Refinery is expected to exacerbate the situation.
