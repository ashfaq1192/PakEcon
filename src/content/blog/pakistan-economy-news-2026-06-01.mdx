## Oil Industry Opposition to Windfall Tax
The oil industry in Pakistan has come out strongly against the proposed windfall tax in the upcoming budget, citing concerns over the impact on their business operations. The Oil Companies Advisory Council (OCAC) has demanded an urgent meeting with the Prime Minister to discuss their concerns and seek his intervention in the matter. The industry is also seeking the release of Rs66bn in withheld price differential claims (PDCs), which they claim are long overdue. Furthermore, the OCAC has urged the Oil and Gas Regulatory Authority (Ogra) to settle all claims by June 8. The opposition to the windfall tax is likely to have significant implications for the Pakistan economy, particularly in terms of the PKR exchange rate and inflation in Pakistan.
