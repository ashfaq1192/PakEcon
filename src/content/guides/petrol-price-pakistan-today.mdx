## How OGRA Sets Petrol Prices and the Impact of Crude Oil Prices
OGRA reviews and adjusts the petrol price Pakistan every fortnight, taking into account various factors such as crude oil prices, the PKR/USD exchange rate, and taxes. According to official data, the current rates of crude oil play a crucial role in determining the petrol rate Pakistan 2026. As per the latest OGRA notification, the petrol price is adjusted based on the movement of crude oil prices in the international market. A decrease in crude oil prices can lead to a reduction in the fuel price today, while an increase can result in higher petrol prices, which means consumers will have to budget more for fuel expenses. Additionally, the PKR/USD exchange rate also affects the petrol price, as a weaker rupee can lead to higher import costs and, subsequently, higher petrol prices.
