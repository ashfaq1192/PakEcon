The petrol rate Pakistan 2026 is influenced by several factors, including the price of crude oil in the international market and the exchange rate of the Pakistani Rupee (PKR) against the US Dollar (USD). When the price of crude oil increases, OGRA adjusts the petrol price accordingly to reflect the change in cost. For instance, according to official data, a 10% rise in crude oil prices can lead to a proportionate increase in petrol prices, which means your monthly fuel expenses will rise.
